GRUBBY PIZZA SHOP

Welcome to Grubby Pizza Shop, the tastiest little pizza joint in town where the cheese drips, the crust crunches, and no slice is ever boring. Whether you're here to grab a quick lunch, fuel up after a long zoo day, or feed a hungry family, Grubby delivers big flavour in every bite. We’re all about bold toppings, saucy layers, and that perfect wood-fired finish — served hot, fast, and with zero pretension.

What is on the show?

  • Chicky Licky BBQ – Tender BBQ chicken, sweetcorn, red onion, and a creamy ranch swirl on top of melted cheddar
  • Hotshot Inferno – Spicy beef, jalapeños, chilli flakes, hot sauce drizzle, and crushed garlic. Not for the faint-hearted.
  • Garden Rebel (Vegan) – Roasted peppers, black olives, mushrooms, vegan cheese, and fresh basil on a dairy-free tomato base. 100% plant-powered

WILD THINGS COFFEE SHOP

Tucked into the heart of the zoo’s bustling food court, the Wild Things Coffee Shop offers a relaxing retreat for animal lovers and families on the go. With shaded outdoor seating and views of nearby enclosures, it’s the perfect spot to take a break between exhibits. The shop blends eco-friendly design with safari-themed décor — think bamboo counters, animal-print accents, and a mural of jungle wildlife. Whether you're recharging after a long walk or grabbing a quick caffeine fix before the next adventure, The Roasted Bean brings specialty coffee and fresh snacks to the wild side.

Menu at a glance

  • Espresso Shot – Bold and rich, served solo or as a double
  • Iced Zoo Brew – Chilled espresso with a splash of milk and chocolate drizzle
  • Savanna Latte – Espresso with steamed milk and a dash of cinnamon
  • Jungle Cold Brew – Smooth, cold-steeped coffee served over ice
  • Giraffe Macchiato – Caramel macchiato with extra foam and a stripe of syrup
